ABOUT US

Established since 2007 as an exporter in the garment industry, QTNP Garment Joint Stock Company has been growing rapidly, with more than US$17 million annually on a FOB basis. Our garments are exported to the US, EU, Australia and APAC markets with main customers such as Quiksilver, Li and Fung, Witt Weiden, Sieh An... The company is willing to expand business cooperation with others from every corner of the world on a win-win basis.
